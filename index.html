EXPERIENCE A CONSULTATION WITH OUR SURGICAL EXPERT, DR MARTIN VANLIERDE!

When Dr Vanlierde does a consultation with a prospective patient, he focuses on three important variables, before deciding to continue with a selected procedure. These variables include being thorough, informative and honest. Meeting with you, he will ensure he understands exactly what you’re looking for from your functional or cosmetic surgery.
